About Trey Oliver

I'm Trey Oliver, a passionate aviator and newly certified Certified Flight Instructor (CFI). I recently earned my CFI certification and secured a job that allows me to use company planes for training private and commercial pilot students.

My goal is to help aspiring pilots like you build the skills and hours needed for a successful career, whether recreational flying or aiming for the airlines. With a focus on safety, fun, and personalized instruction, I'll guide you every step of the way.

Certifications & Experience

  • FAA Certified Flight Instructor (CFI)
  • Private Pilot License
  • Instrument Rating
  • Building toward Commercial Pilot and Airline Transport Pilot
  • Access to a fleet of reliable aircraft for lessons
