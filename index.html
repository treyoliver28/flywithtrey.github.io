About Me

I'm Trey Oliver, a certified CFI with a passion for aviation. I’m employed by Air America at Daytona International Airport (KDAB), where I train private and commercial students to build the skills and flight hours needed for airline careers or recreational flying.

In addition to my work at Daytona, I also provide instruction at Jacksonville Executive Airport (KCRG). My focus is always on safe, fun, and personalized training.

Flight Instruction Services

As a dedicated CFI, I offer flexible, personalized training using aircraft like Cessna 172s and more. I focus on real-world flying to prepare you for success—whether recreational or career-bound. With my own growing flight hours, I'm invested in your progress!

I currently offer the following services:

  • Private Pilot Certificate Training – Ground school, flight lessons, and checkride prep.
  • Commercial Pilot Certificate Training – Advanced maneuvers, cross-country, and hour-building.
  • Flight Reviews – Refresh and stay current.
  • Aerial Photography – Take the most stunning photos from the sky.

Feel free to reach out to discuss your flight training goals. Note: I can also provide instruction in your own aircraft.
